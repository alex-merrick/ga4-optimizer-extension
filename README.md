Make Google Anlytics usable again. GA4 Optimizer is a private Chrome extension that fixes the most frustrating parts of the Google Analytics 4 interface. Built by and for Data Analysts and Marketers.

🔥 TOP FEATURES THAT WILL SAVE YOU HOURS
✦ Bring Back Exit Pages: GA4 removed the standard Exit Pages report. We brought it back. Generate a pre-configured Exit Pages report template with a single click.
✦ Fix Shared Links ("No Access" Error): Tired of getting "Missing Permissions" when a colleague shares a GA4 link? Most of the time this is due to multipe Google accounts being used. We automatically detect this error and help you try with your other logged-in Google accounts.
✦ Custom Definitions Copy/Paste: Setting up a new property? Effortlessly migrate custom dimensions, custom metrics, and calculated metrics between GA4 properties. An absolute lifesaver for agencies and power users!
✦ 1-Click Date Comparisons & Presets: Stop fighting the calendar. Compare MoM or YoY instantly, including a highly accurate "matching day of week" alignment. Plus, quick presets like Last 7 Days or Last Month.
✦ Sticky Report Headers: Never lose track of which metric column you are looking at when scrolling through long Standard Reports.

📊 REPORTING & ANALYSIS ENHANCEMENTS
✦ Percentage Change Highlighter: Make key trends pop. Visually identify significant positive or negative metric changes when comparing dates using custom color-coded thresholds.
✦ On-the-Fly Calculated Metrics: Create and save custom rates (like 'Exit Rate' or 'Conversion Rate') directly within your Standard Reports and Explorations.
✦ Automatic Statistical Significance: Instantly know if your A/B test results are trustworthy with built-in confidence calculations right inside your reports.
✦ Δ% Share Change: Go beyond raw percentages. See the change in a metric's contribution to the total (Δ%Shr) to get a much clearer picture of performance shifts.

💡 EXPLORATION POWER TOOLS (Hover & Discover)
✦ Maximize View: Get your screen real estate back. Instantly expand Freeform Explorations to the maximum columns/rows possible, complete with intelligent pagination controls and keyboard shortcuts.
✦ Percentage of Column Total: Simply hover over any cell to quickly see a row's contribution to the column total. No clicking required.
✦ Performance vs. 'All Users': Quickly assess any segment's performance relative to the 'All Users' baseline on hover.
✦ Collapsible Panels: Maximize your analysis workspace with a quick toggle to collapse the "Variables" and "Tab Settings" panels.
✦ Detailed Results Shortcut: Force Explorations to use "More detailed results" (less sampling) via a quick keyboard shortcut.

⚙️ WORKFLOW & UI UPGRADES
✦ Advanced Click-to-Copy: 1-click to copy data from any cell. Use a double-click for full text content, and a triple-click in Explorations to copy rich hover-tooltip data.
✦ Sticky Quick Calculator: A handy, draggable calculator widget is always accessible for quick rate and uplift calculations on any page.
✦ Highlight Sampling Icon: A subtle pulsing animation draws your attention to the data quality icon if your report is secretly using sampled data.
✦ Line Graph Customizations: Customize your line chart colors to match your reporting and presentation needs.
✦ International Formatting: Full support for international users that use comma number decimal formatting (e.g., 1.234,56).

🔒 PRIVATE AND SECURE
