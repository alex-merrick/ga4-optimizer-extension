# GA4 Optimizer

**Make Google Analytics usable again.**

GA4 Optimizer is a **private Chrome extension** that fixes the most frustrating parts of the Google Analytics 4 interface. Built *by* and *for* **Data Analysts and Marketers**.

---

## 🔥 Top Features That Will Save You Hours

- **Bring Back Exit Pages**  
  GA4 removed the standard Exit Pages report. We brought it back. Generate a pre-configured Exit Pages report template with a single click.

- **Fix Shared Links (“No Access” Error)**  
  Tired of getting “Missing Permissions” when a colleague shares a GA4 link? This often happens when multiple Google accounts are in use. GA4 Optimizer automatically detects the issue and helps you retry with other logged-in accounts.

- **Custom Definitions Copy / Paste**  
  Setting up a new property? Effortlessly migrate **custom dimensions**, **custom metrics**, and **calculated metrics** between GA4 properties. An absolute lifesaver for agencies and power users.

- **1‑Click Date Comparisons & Presets**  
  Stop fighting the calendar. Instantly compare **MoM** or **YoY**, including a highly accurate *matching day-of-week* alignment. Plus, quick presets like **Last 7 Days** or **Last Month**.

- **Sticky Report Headers**  
  Never lose track of which metric column you’re viewing when scrolling through long Standard Reports.

---

## 📊 Reporting & Analysis Enhancements

- **Percentage Change Highlighter**  
  Make key trends pop. Visually identify significant positive or negative metric changes with custom color‑coded thresholds.

- **On‑the‑Fly Calculated Metrics**  
  Create and save custom rates (such as *Exit Rate* or *Conversion Rate*) directly within Standard Reports and Explorations.

- **Automatic Statistical Significance**  
  Instantly know whether your A/B test results are trustworthy with built‑in confidence calculations inside your reports.

- **Δ% Share Change**  
  Go beyond raw percentages. See how a metric’s contribution to the total changes (Δ%Shr) for clearer performance insights.

---

## 💡 Exploration Power Tools (Hover & Discover)

- **Maximize View**  
  Instantly expand Freeform Explorations to the maximum number of columns and rows, with intelligent pagination and keyboard shortcuts.

- **Percentage of Column Total**  
  Hover over any cell to see its contribution to the column total — no clicks required.

- **Performance vs. “All Users”**  
  Quickly evaluate any segment’s performance relative to the full user baseline.

- **Collapsible Panels**  
  Maximize your workspace with one‑click toggles for the **Variables** and **Tab Settings** panels.

- **Detailed Results Shortcut**  
  Force Explorations to use *More detailed results* (less sampling) via a keyboard shortcut.

## ⚙️ Workflow & UI Upgrades

- **Advanced Click‑to‑Copy**  
  - Single‑click: copy cell values  
  - Double‑click: copy full text  
  - Triple‑click (Explorations): copy rich hover‑tooltip data

- **Sticky Quick Calculator**  
  A draggable calculator widget is always available for quick rate and uplift calculations.

- **Sampling Icon Highlight**  
  A subtle pulsing animation draws attention when your report is silently using sampled data.

- **Line Graph Customizations**  
  Customize line chart colors to match your reporting or presentation needs.

- **International Formatting**  
  Full support for international number formats (e.g. `1.234,56`).
