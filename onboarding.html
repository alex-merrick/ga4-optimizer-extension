Get started by pinning the extension!

Get started by pinning the extension! GA4 Optimizer adds powerful features like Click-to-Copy and insightful Row Percentages to make your GA4 analysis smoother. Follow these simple steps to pin it to your Chrome toolbar:

  1. Click the Puzzle Piece icon (🧩) in your Chrome toolbar (usually top-right).
  2. Find "GA4 Optimizer" in the list of extensions.
  3. Click the Pin icon (📌) next to it.

That's it! The GA4 Optimizer icon will now always be visible for quick access to settings.
